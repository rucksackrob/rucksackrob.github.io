The Relationship between Tuition and Teacher Salary

You might think that a high tuition is helping to pay elite professors and maintain the quality of teaching. But the data doesn’t bear that out. Regardless of region or ownership status of the university, there is little relationship between the tuition you pay and the salary the professor receives.

Select a region in the legend to see if this relationship changes depending on where in the country schools are located.

Click on "region" to clear the filters.

Mouse over a data point to get school-specific information.
